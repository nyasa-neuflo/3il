FAQ

Is my reservation instantly confirmed after booking?

Yes, once you complete your booking and payment, your reservation is confirmed instantly. You will receive a confirmation email with all your booking details shortly after.

Can I update or cancel my reservation after booking?

Please make sure to check the cancellation policy of the hotel you booked, as rules can vary.

Are the prices and availability always accurate?

We work closely with hotel partners to ensure all pricing and availability data is accurate and up to date. However, in rare cases of sudden changes on the hotel’s side, updates may take a few moments to reflect. Always double-check the final summary before confirming your booking.

What should I do if I face issues during booking or don’t receive a confirmation?

If you experience any issues during the booking process or do not receive a confirmation email, please contact our customer support team right away. Our support is available 24 hours a day to help you resolve the issue and ensure your reservation is properly confirmed.
